## About Google Antigravity

Google Antigravity is an IDE that places AI agents within your regular code-writing workflow. It looks just like any other editor, particularly VS Code, and hence familiar. It has a different operation under the hood.

Antigravity does not just predict the next line of code but, instead, plans what to build, creates the files, and even tests the output. It exploits the [Gemini]( models of Google and connects applications like the editor, terminal, and an inbuilt browser, which the artificial intelligence may operate to test or browse the internet.

It is on the preview stage and is only available for its personal use currently; thus, it can be modified. The point is that developers can perceive the AI as a mini-assistant that operates on several surfaces simultaneously. Antigravity presents the AI activities through dashboards, as well as plans, logs, and recorded tests. Instead of working silently, it gives sufficient details that users can monitor the progress.
